WHY COUNTDOWN?

The main purpose of a countdown timer is to create a sense of urgency and give the feeling that “time is running out.”
In addition to this, businesses also use countdown timers to count down to when a special discount or offer will become available.
You can use them in various ways, by counting down to a specific event, counting up to an event, or even letting shoppers know the time left to receive free shipping on an item.
By creating a sense of urgency, a countdown widget can make consumers complete a purchase.
